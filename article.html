Shavkat Mirziyoyev sternly warned Governors of all levels

21:00 / 29.03.2022

On May 19, a videoconference was held on additional measures to support entrepreneurship.

According to the press service of the head of state, due to the pandemic in Uzbekistan, the number of poor and unemployed will increase, while family incomes will decrease. The meeting discussed additional measures and priority tasks in this direction.

First of all, serious attention is paid to guaranteeing the rights of entrepreneurs and the inviolability of their property.

“I warn all khokims. If the property of at least one entrepreneur is seized illegally, the hokim will not get off with a resignation, but will also answer before the law,” Shavkat Mirziyoyev emphasized.

Competent departments were instructed to strengthen the responsibility for violation of the inviolability of property, to ensure the full implementation of court decisions on compensation for the property of entrepreneurs.

According to the instructions of the President, business activity in each district and city will be analyzed on a monthly basis.

The business ombudsman will submit information to the government about the khokims of districts and cities who violate the rights and inviolability of the property of entrepreneurs.

The Cabinet of Ministers will consider the issue of compliance with the position of khokims of cities and regions who do not solve the problems of entrepreneurs and do not work with them sufficiently.
